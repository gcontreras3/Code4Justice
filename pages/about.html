Find a Fridge Near You

Food, a Human Right

Consider all the food that gets left behind in everyday industrys. Everything from grocery stores to left over stock from local restaurants, they all end up allocated to the dumpster out back. Our mission is to inspire and empower everyone to take action against food waste. We know that to live and breathe this every day, we need to turn our words into actions. With this in mind we have set out a new ambition - to contribute in every way we can to building the global food waste movement. It's only when we all come together to fight food waste, that we'll be able to generate a positive change in society.

Serving Over 250+ Communities

Use the map above to find a fridge near you. We have established fridges and community hubs in over 250+ communites since 2022. We source our food from grocery stores to left over stock from restautrants. We keep our fridges powered by donations and change rotation every 2 days to keep food supply safe. We also source fresh produce from community gardens that we provide compost to in order to continue to grow and manage the gardens. Seek out the about page if you're looking to host a fridge of your own.

All Hands on Deck

Most of our fresh produce is sourced from local gardens or farms. We're comprised of over 500 volunteers that help maintain all 250 locations. Click the contact page if you're interested in becoming a volunteer. The surplus food is only one part of the equation. Our real heartbeat are our volunteers and all those who host our fridges. Especially during uncertain times where inflation prices began to rise. Not only do we provide security to those facing situations we were once in, but we're strengenthing our communities for the better.

QuickBytes
